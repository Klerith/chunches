01

Hello, here's the short version

Chunches ("the app", "we", "us") is a mobile app made for families who want to organize shared shopping lists, recipes and events in one place. This page explains what info we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

02

What we collect

Stuff you give us

  • Account: name, email, and password (or your Google / Apple Sign-In credentials).
  • Profile: a profile photo, if you feel like adding one.
  • Family content: shopping lists, items, recipes, ingredients, steps, calendar events and your family's name.

Stuff collected automatically

  • Usage data: general app interactions we use to spot bugs and improve things.
  • Push token: so we can send event reminders and family updates — only if you opt in.

Optional integrations

  • Device calendar: if you allow it, the app can read and write events to your native calendar. We don't keep a copy on our servers beyond what's needed to sync.
03

How we use it

Purpose Legal basis
Create and manage your account Performance of contract
Sync content between family members in real time Performance of contract
Send push notifications for events and reminders Consent
Detect and fix technical errors Legitimate interest
Comply with legal obligations Legal obligation

! We don't run ads on your data, and we don't sell it to anyone. Promise.

04

Who sees what

  • Within your family: what you create (lists, recipes, events) is visible to all members of your family group inside the app.
  • Service providers: we use Supabase for database, auth and storage, and Firebase for push notifications. Both act as data processors under confidentiality agreements.
  • Authorities: we may disclose info when we're legally required to.

That's it — we don't share your personal info with anyone else.

05

How we keep it safe

  • Your data lives on Supabase servers with encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Profile photos are stored in secure storage with per-user access controls.
  • Access to family data is enforced by Row Level Security (RLS) policies right at the database level.
06

How long we keep it

We keep your info while your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal content are wiped within 30 days.
  • Shared family content (lists, recipes, events) may stay visible to other members until they delete it.
07

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Correct anything that's wrong.
  • Delete your account and the data tied to it.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications anytime, from your device settings.

To use any of these, just drop us a message — contact info is at the bottom.

08

A note about kids

Chunches is built for families and we don't knowingly collect data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you find that a minor has shared data with us, let us know and we'll remove it.

09

When this changes

If we make significant changes, we'll let you know with an in-app notification or by email. Continuing to use the app after that means you accept the updated policy.
