01

Introduction

Chunches ("the app", "we", "us") is a mobile application designed for families to organize shared shopping lists, recipes, and events in one place. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

02

Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account: name, email address, and password (or credentials from Google or Apple Sign-In).
  • Profile: profile photo (optional).
  • Family content: shopping lists, items, recipes, ingredients, steps, calendar events, and your family name.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data: general app interactions used to detect errors and improve the experience.
  • Push notification token: to send you event reminders and family updates (only if you grant permission).

2.3 Optional Integrations

  • Device calendar: if you authorize it, the app may read and write events to your native calendar. This data is not stored on our servers beyond what is necessary for synchronization.
03

How We Use Your Information

Purpose Legal basis
Create and manage your account Performance of contract

We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it to third parties.

04

Data Sharing

  • Within your family: content you create (lists, recipes, events) is visible to all members of your family group inside the app.
  • Service providers: we use Supabase as our database, authentication, and storage platform, and Firebase for push notifications. Both act as data processors under confidentiality agreements.
  • Authorities: we may disclose information when required by law.

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

05

Storage and Security

  • Data is stored on Supabase servers with encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Profile photos are stored in secure storage with per-user access controls.
  • Access to family data is enforced by Row Level Security (RLS) policies at the database level.
06

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal content are deleted within 30 days.
  • Shared family content (lists, recipes, events) may remain visible to other members until they delete it.
07

Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time via your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address listed at the end of this document.

08

Children's Privacy

Chunches is intended for families and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you discover that a minor has provided data without such consent, please contact us so we can remove it.

09

Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes through an in-app notification or by email. Continued use of the app after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
